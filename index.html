About Us

Turning Moments into Movements

🎥 Cinematography 📡 Live Production 🤖 AI-Powered ⚡ Fast Delivery

Precious Live Media is a professional media production company based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. We exist to solve one problem: your event deserves to look and feel world-class — not like an afterthought streamed from a smartphone.

We started because we kept seeing events with incredible content let down by poor production. Pastors speaking to empty online rooms, corporate events that looked amateur, performances nobody could see properly. We decided to fix that.

Today, we bring broadcast-grade live streaming, multi-camera production, cinematography, and AI-powered workflows to every event — big or small. From intimate church services to large-scale corporate conferences, we show up with the right gear, the right crew, and the right plan.

Our promise is simple: zero technical excuses, maximum impact. You bring the vision; we bring the production.
